Privacy Policy for Plasview Extension

Effective Date: 13.09.2025

Plasview (“we”, “our”, or “the Extension”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use our Chrome Extension.

1. Information We Collect

Plasview only collects information necessary to provide its functionality, including:

  • User-submitted content: Any text or data you select or highlight in web pages while using the extension, which is sent to our backend for processing.
  • Anonymous usage data (optional): Aggregate, non-identifiable data to help improve the extension (only if you enable this feature).

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email, or login credentials unless you voluntarily provide it through the extension interface.

2. How We Use Collected Data

We use the data solely for the purpose of:

  • Processing content or requests made through the extension.
  • Improving the performance, stability, and features of the extension.

Data is not sold, shared, or used for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored securely on our servers and only for as long as necessary to process your requests. We implement standard security measures to protect data from unauthorized access.

4. Third-Party Services

Our extension may interact with external services to provide its functionality. We do not provide these services with any personally identifiable information unless required to process your request.

5. User Control

You can:

  • Stop using the extension at any time.
  • Clear any local data stored by the extension.
  • Request deletion of any data stored on our backend by contacting us at plasviewextension@gmail.com.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be effective upon posting the updated policy in the extension or on our website.

7. Contact

For questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at plasviewextension@gmail.com.
